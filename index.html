You've arrived at Responsive Flavor, the place where your web layouts will have to prove that are responsive worthy!

Go ahead give it a spin and check if it supports the various resolution web has to offer.

How it works

  • Add your URL to the input field
  • Click Go!
  • Embrace your magnificent work
  • Use the slider at the bottom to change its resolution

Features

  • Supports smartphone, tablet and desktop resolutions, available at the top menu bar.
  • Dynamic control of the viewport through the bottom slider
  • The minimum width available is 320px and maximum is 1980px (yes, a horizontal scrollbar may appear :P )
Builted with:
  • Foundation 4
  • jQuery UI
